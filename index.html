Japanese in a nutshell
This website was created to give a good idea of how the Japanese language uses and mixes four different writing systems (read alphabet)
The Four Different Alphabets
HIRAGANA. This is the basic and most important alphabet to know.
KATAKANA. The second writing system taught to students.
KANJI. Or Chinese characters. Those are the meat of the written language.
ROMAJI. Or roman characters. Mostly used for international purposes.
